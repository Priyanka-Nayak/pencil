Introduction

Reimagining the publishing experience design of an intuitive author platform and reader app for The Pencil App

Many writers struggle with the lack of transparency, limited creative control, and the need to manage their content across multiple disconnected platforms. The current publishing landscape presents a complex and often confusing user experience for aspiring authors, making it challenging to navigate the process of creating, publishing, and distributing their work.

Research

Pencil is a self-publishing platform designed to empower authors to write, edit, publish, and distribute their books globally. Despite its innovative mission, authors found the platform challenging to navigate due to a steep learning curve and limited features for collaboration and marketing. Our team was assigned to redesign Pencil to provide a seamless experience for authors by simplifying the publishing process, enabling collaboration, and enhancing analytics for better decision-making.

Analysis

*Obligatory picture of the team with a wall full of sticky notes*

The data collected through the first four methods—observations, surveys, interviews, and social media analysis—centered on understanding the needs, preferences, and pain points of authors using the Pencil platform. For this data, we employed affinity diagramming to identify patterns and organize insights. Usability testing was focused on uncovering specific challenges with the platform’s interface and features, and this data was analyzed separately to ensure actionable outcomes for the redesign.

Research Findings
We relied on these primary findings to lead us to the ideation phase.


  • ○     Authors appreciate simple, intuitive tools that streamline the writing and publishing process.
  • ○     There is a growing desire for stronger community features to connect authors with readers and peers.
  • ○     Mobile optimization is a must-have, allowing authors to work seamlessly across devices.
  • ○     Authors value marketing and analytics support to better track their performance and growth.
  • ○     Content creation tools are seen as an area for enhancement to improve the writing experience.
  • ○     Transparency in earnings is highly valued, helping authors feel more confident in their income.
  • ○     Authors actively seek interaction features to encourage deeper connections with their audience.

Ideation

After multiple brainstorming sessions, we shortlisted five design ideas to enhance the Pencil publishing experience.


Collaborative Writer’s Room

  1. An interactive workspace allowing authors to collaborate in real-time.
  2. Authors can simultaneously edit documents, leave inline feedback, and conduct brainstorming sessions.
  3. Enhanced by live video chat integration for virtual writing workshops.

Intuitive Analytics Dashboard

  1. Provides authors with easy-to-understand analytics about their readership and book performance.
  2. Includes insights like reader demographics, popular chapters, and revenue breakdown.
  3. Allows authors to make data-driven decisions to improve audience engagement and marketing strategies.

Seamless Cross-Device Writing

  1. A unified experience enabling authors to transition effortlessly between desktop and mobile.
  2. Automatic synchronization of progress, edits, and notes across all devices.
  3. Designed specifically for authors on-the-go, facilitating productivity anytime, anywhere.

Interactive Community Hub

  1. A dedicated space within the app for authors and readers to engage and interact.
  2. Features include author Q&As, live discussions, and reader forums to build stronger community connections.
  3. Encourages reader engagement through polls, surveys, and interactive content.

Enhanced Marketing Toolkit

  1. A comprehensive set of integrated tools within the platform designed to empower authors in effectively promoting their books.
  2. Features include social media integration for scheduling and sharing updates, customizable press kits tailored to authors' unique brands and genres, and targeted email campaigns to engage readers and subscribers directly.
  3. Supports authors in maximizing outreach through streamlined promotional resources and personalized marketing content.

Stakeholder Feedback
We presented these concepts to Pencil Publishing stakeholders and a diverse group of authors for feedback.


Stakeholders favored the Collaborative Writer’s Room for its potential to significantly boost user retention and platform activity.

Authors expressed strong enthusiasm for both the Intuitive Analytics Dashboard and the Interactive Community Hub due to their direct impact on marketing and audience building.

Based on these insights, we chose to integrate essential aspects from all five ideas to create a comprehensive solution.

Proposed Solution

The final solution became an integrated, intuitive publishing ecosystem designed around author collaboration, detailed analytics, seamless mobility, and community engagement.

Collaborative Writing Suite:
Enables real-time collaborative editing, feedback, and virtual workshops.

Enhanced Analytics Dashboard:
A streamlined interface that delivers insightful, actionable data to help authors better understand their audience.

Cross-platform Accessibility:
Effortless syncing of author dashboard and analytics data across web and mobile devices, ensuring authors can manage their content seamlessly.

Community Engagement Hub:
A vibrant platform where authors can directly engage readers through posts: polls, surveys, and interactive content, enhancing reader-author interactions.

Integrated Marketing Toolkit:
Comprehensive promotional tools, including social media integration, customizable press kits, and targeted email campaigns.

The redesigned Pencil app provides authors with end-to-end tools to write, publish, and grow their readership effectively, empowering them with transparency and enhanced creative control.

Testing

We conducted extensive testing with expert authors, stakeholders from Pencil Publishing, and new authors unfamiliar with the platform.

Cognitive Walkthroughs
Participants included five publishing experts and Pencil Publishing executives.
Tasks involved navigating collaboration features, interpreting analytics data, transitioning between devices, and engaging in community forums.

Moderated Usability Testing
Session 1: Group of three authors collaborating on a shared manuscript.
Session 2: Individual authors exploring analytics features and community engagement tools.




Feedback
The major insights from the testing phase:



What Worked Well?

  • ○     Real-time collaboration was praised for significantly streamlining co-authoring and peer-review processes.
  • ○     Authors found the simplified analytics dashboard highly effective for understanding and growing their audience.
  • ○     Cross-device functionality was seamless, encouraging authors to use Pencil across multiple scenarios.
  • ○     Community engagement features greatly improved the sense of connection among authors and readers.

What Could Be Improved?

  • ●     Initial onboarding for new collaboration tools required additional clarity and guided walkthroughs.
  • ●     Authors desired more personalized analytics insights tailored specifically to their genre.
  • ●    Mobile app required minor optimizations for easier access to community notifications.
  • ●     Enhanced privacy options were requested within community forums for sensitive or niche discussions.
